Quick restart sequence (short version) — what to do after laptop reboot

If you just want the minimal steps to get the simulation back up and test:

Admin tab:

cd "M:\CCNS project work\project\iot-botnet-simulation"
docker-compose up -d
docker ps --format "table {{.Names}}\t{{.Image}}\t{{.Status}}"


Wait ~5–10s, then confirm C2 connected:

docker logs -f iot-botnet-simulation-c2-1 --tail=50
# expect: MQTT on_connect rc=0


MQTT tab — confirm telemetry one-shot:

docker-compose exec mosquitto mosquitto_sub -t 'devices/data' -C 1


Publish attack and watch:

MQTT tab:

docker-compose exec mosquitto mosquitto_pub -t 'c2/commands' -m 'do_attack'


Watch C2 tab and victim tab logs for GETs.
